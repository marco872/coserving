Governments/Investors

* Investors provide capital for developments using Liquidity Pools, guaranteed by Government funds.
* To show interest in Co-Serving platform, Investors and Governments must commmit their funds.
* They do so by uploading in the main menu, after having registered, the capital amount.
* Investors upload in "Add-Liquidity", Governments upload in "Add-Collateral".
* Example Collateral Application, the following data must be uploaded:
State:.../ City:.../Ministry:.../Pool:.../Amount:.../
* Example Liquidity Pool Application, the following data must be uploaded:
Type:.../ L.P.name:.../L.P. value:.../Status:.../Smart Contracts:.../
* Investors and Governments follow projects developments connecting to operating trading desk.
