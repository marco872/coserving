WHITE PAPER:"How to create a blockchain platform to eliminate additional third-party extra-costs, such as bureaucratic banking fees and developers' speculation, in Real Estate projects."
STEP 1:

Governments and Institutional funds provide investment to create Private Liquidity Pools (pLP).

pLPs use fiat capital to finance Design/Build (D/B) projects for the community.

Tokens (FBM token) generate a retribution system for the communityâ€™s users of the D/B projects.

STEP 2:

D/B projects use a business model based on Fair Profit Distribution* (FPD).

FPD revenues return the capital invested + interest into the pLP.

*(FPD is a "margin scale" that sets the maximum and minimum profit of D/B projects' activities: For example: Interest Fiat capital: 3.5 to 6.5 %)

STEP 3:

All FPD revenue , at first, it will be used to fill-up the pLP until it is fully satisfied.

Subsequently, pLP will receive only FPD

The remaining amount will be used to create Public Liquidity Pool

The pLP funds will be used to invest in another D/B project. And so on by creating a self-regenerating financial system to finance the sustainable expansion of urban housing.

STEP 4:

The Public Liquidity Pool (puLP) increases the market value of the FBM token.

FBM token represents the identity of the entire Peer to Peer (blockchain)

STEP 5:

The Blockchain distributed ledger uses Co-Serving native dapp, to promote and manage D/B project activities.

STEP 6:

Co-Serving Functions related to Private Liquidity Pool:

A- To collect necessary data to develop a project:

Property data./ Design data./ Structural data./ Construction data./

B- To Collect Liquidity Pool data.

C- To Connect A&B through an algorithm.

D- To Connect multiple stakeholders.

E- To Reconciliate different data.

F- To Transfer value.

G- To Maintain immutability of the data.

STEP 7:

Co-Serving Functions related to Public Liquidity Pool:

To List the activities of D/B projects.

To make bookings and payments of these activities.

To manage D/B projects.

To distribute revenue to pLP and puLP.

To track Token value through Smart Contract: Standard3

To show Token offers trhough Smart Contract Standard3
