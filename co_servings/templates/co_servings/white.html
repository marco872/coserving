How Co-Serving Works:
"Co-Serving is a Real Estate Platform to create projects without banking costs, developers' speculative profit, and unnecessary brokerage fees."
INVESTORS:

Governments and Institutional funds provide capital using (Main navigation bar) dropdown Downloads , item : "Add-Liquidity"

Co-Serving projects use a business model based on Fair Profit's Distribution* (FPD).

FPD is a "margin scale" that sets the maximum and minimum return to the investment between 3.5 to 6.5 %

OWNERS:

Property Owners provide Design-Build Projects selected in Co-Serving Catalogue(available in the navigation bar) using (Main navigation bar) dropdown Downloads, item: "Add-Projects"

The D/B projects include architectural, consultants, approval fees and all construction costs to deliver turnkey developments.

TENANTS:

Tenants become Member by using ( Tenants' navigation bar) dropdown Qualification, item: "Membership" to have access to benefits offered by Co-Serving projects.

to meet the minimum qualification necessary to become a memeber, rules and benefits are available in Tenants'navigation bar Qualification, item: "Hosting Rules"

GUESTS:

Guets become Member by using ( Guests' navigation bar) dropdown Qualification, item: "Membership" to have access to the transparency process offered by Co-Serving projects.

to meet the minimum qualification necessary to become a memeber, rules and benefits are available in Guests'navigation bar Qualification, item: "Guest Rules"
