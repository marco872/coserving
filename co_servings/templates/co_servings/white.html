How Co-Serving Works:
"Co-Serving is a Real Estate Platform to create projects without banking costs, developers' speculative profit, and unnecessary brokerage fees."
INVESTORS:

Governments and Institutional funds provide investment to create Private Liquidity Pools (pLP).

pLPs use fiat capital to finance Design/Build (D/B) projects for the community.

Tokens (FBM token) generate a retribution system for the communityâ€™s users of the D/B projects.

PROPERTY OWNERS:

P.Os Upload D/B Projects selected in the Co-Serving Catalogue.

The D/B projects include architectural, consultants, approval fees and all construction costs to deliver turnkey developments.

TENANTS:

Tenants must accept the ThreeWay Contract rules in order to qualify for the benefits offered by Co-Serving projects.

ThreeWay Contract rules and benefits are available when clicking the Co-Serving Token in the Home page.

FPD:

D/B projects use a business model based on Fair Profit's Distribution* (FPD).

FPD revenues return the capital invested + interest into the pLP.

*(FPD is a "margin scale" that sets the maximum and minimum profit of D/B projects' activities: For example: Interest Fiat capital: 3.5 to 6.5 %)
