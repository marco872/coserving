WHITE PAPER:
STEP 1:

Governments and Institutional funds provide investment to create Private Liquidity Pools (pLP).

pLPs use the portion of fiat capital to finance Design/Build (D/B) projects for the community.

And uses the token's portion to generate a retribution system(FBM token) for the communityâ€™s users of the D/B projects.

STEP 2:

D/B projects, generated with pLPs fiat capital, create revenues using a business model based on Fair Profit Distribution* (FPD).

These revenues will return the capital invested + interest into the pLP.

*(FPD is a margin regulator that sets the maximum and minimum values of the profit of the activities of D/B projects: For example: Interest Fiat capital: 3.5 to 6.5 %)

STEP 3:

All profits from D/B activities will be used to fill the pLP until it is fully satisfied.

Subsequently, pLP will receive only FPD

The remaining amount will be used to create Public Liquidity Pool

The pLP funds will be used to invest in another D/B project. And so on by creating a self-regenerating financial system to finance the sustainable expansion of urban housing.

STEP 4:

The Public Liquidity Pool (puLP) increases the market value of the FBM token.

FBM token represents the identity of the entire Peer to Peer (blockchain)

This blockchain supports a real estate platform that eliminates additional third-party extra-costs, such as bureaucratic banking fees and developer profits.

STEP 6:

The Blockchain distributed ledger uses Co-Serving native dapp.

Private Liquidity Pools and Public Liquidity Pools generate financial activities.

STEP 7:

Functions related to Public Liquidity Pool:

To List the activities of D/B projects.

To make bookings and payments of these activities.

To manage D/B projects.
