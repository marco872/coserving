How Co-Serving Works:
"Co-Serving is a Real Estate Platform to create projects without banking costs, developers' speculative profit, and unnecessary brokerage fees. Projects connected to a Daily Trading Exchange for everybody to partecipate in clear Real Estate investments. Co-Serving also has its own token."
Governments:

Commit capital to cover Liquidity Pools using (Main navigation bar) dropdown Downloads , item : "Add-Collateral" and add the funds using AAVE DEFI protocol

FUNDERS:

Investment funds commit capital using (Main navigation bar) dropdown Downloads , item : "Add-Liquidity" and add the funds using Pancake Swap platform and Metamask Wallet directly linked at (Real Estate Exchange navigation bar) item: "Trading Desk"

Co-Serving projects use a business model based on Fair Profit's Distribution* (FPD).

FPD is a "margin scale" that sets the maximum and minimum return to the investment between 3.5 to 6.5 % compound interest.

INVESTORS:

Investors register by using ( Investors' navigation bar) dropdown Qualification, item: "Membership" to have access to the transparency process offered by Co-Serving projects. And to add the investment use Pancake Swap platform and Metamask Wallet directly linked at (Real Estate Exchange navigation bar) item: "Trading Desk"

to meet the minimum qualification necessary to become a memeber, rules and benefits are available in Investors'navigation bar Qualification, item: "InvestorsRules"

OWNERS:

Property Owners provide Design-Build Projects selected in Co-Serving Catalogue(available in the navigation bar) using (Main navigation bar) dropdown Downloads, item: "Add-Projects"

The D/B projects include architectural, consultants, approval fees and all construction costs to deliver turnkey developments.

TENANTS:

Tenants become Member by using ( Tenants' navigation bar) dropdown Qualification, item: "Membership" to have access to benefits offered by Co-Serving projects.

to meet the minimum qualification necessary to become a memeber, rules and benefits are available in Tenants'navigation bar Qualification, item: "Hosting Rules"

GUESTS:

Guests become Member by using ( Guests' navigation bar) dropdown Qualification, item: "Membership" to have access to the transparency process offered by Co-Serving projects.

to meet the minimum qualification necessary to become a memeber, rules and benefits are available in Guests'navigation bar Qualification, item: "Guest Rules"
